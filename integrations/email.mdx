# Email Integration

Email brings longer-form support conversations into the same Woes inbox as live
chat and Discord. It is useful for account follow-up, async developer support,
and survey delivery.

## What Email Adds

- Inbound customer support threads.
- Operator replies from the Woes inbox.
- Email-delivered NPS or CSAT surveys.
- Customer and account context tied to conversation history.

## Setup Path

1. Review [Email](/channels/email).
2. Confirm domain and sender configuration.
3. Send a test inbound email.
4. Reply from Woes and confirm the customer receives it.
5. Enable email surveys only after the support flow is working.
