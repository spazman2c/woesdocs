# Email Integration

Email is a Woes support channel for teams that want traditional support addresses to land in the same workspace as live chat and Discord.

## Status

**Available now.** Email has a channel settings surface and participates in the shared support workspace model.

## What it does

Email lets teams:

- Receive inbound support messages through configured email surfaces.
- Reply from Woes while keeping the conversation tied to the customer record.
- Use the same inbox, statuses, tags, assignment, SLA, surveys, and analytics concepts as other support channels.
- Keep email support connected to API context and agent workflows instead of handling it in a separate mailbox.

## Where it appears in Woes

- **Workspace Settings -\> Channels -\> Email** configures email behavior.
- **Inbox** shows email conversations beside live chat and Discord.
- **Automations** can route and tag issues based on channel and issue fields.
- **Surveys** can use email delivery where configured.

## What to configure

- Sending and receiving domain setup.
- Reply behavior and forwarding rules.
- Agent selector or routing context where applicable.
- Survey and notification behavior.

## Operational notes

Email should be treated as a channel value over the same conversation/message model, not a separate ticketing system. That keeps customer history, routing, and analytics consistent across Woes.
