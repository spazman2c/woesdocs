# Slack Connector

Slack is a planned team-workflow connector for support alerts and escalation visibility.

## Status

**Planned connector.** The Settings catalog may show Slack as planned, but Woes should not present a working Slack install action until OAuth, workspace mapping, delivery routes, permission checks, retry behavior, and audit logging are implemented.

## What it will do

Slack should help support teams keep important Woes events visible where teammates already coordinate:

- Send SLA breach alerts to selected Slack channels.
- Notify teams when urgent issues are created or reassigned.
- Post automation-generated alerts from Woes workflows.
- Link back to the relevant Woes conversation, issue, or client.
- Optionally support channel-specific routing rules later.

## Product fit

Slack is best as a notification and escalation surface, not a replacement for the Woes inbox. Operators should still resolve customer conversations in Woes so assignment, status, SLA, audit, and analytics stay coherent.

## Implementation requirements

Before Slack becomes active, Woes needs:

- Slack OAuth install and reconnect flow.
- Workspace-to-Slack-team mapping.
- Channel picker with safe permission checks.
- Message delivery, retry, and error states.
- Automation action support.
- Tenant-scoped activity logs and secret redaction.

## Early recipes

- Notify `#support-triage` when an issue breaches first-response SLA.
- Post urgent customer issues to a product support channel.
- Notify the assigned team when a Discord support thread becomes high priority.
