# Webhooks Connector

Webhooks are a planned connector for sending Woes support events to customer-owned systems.

## Status

**Planned connector.** Webhooks are visible as a product direction, but they should remain disabled until signing, retries, delivery logs, permissions, and failure behavior are implemented.

## What it will do

A webhook connector could send events such as:

- Conversation created or updated.
- Issue created, updated, solved, or reopened.
- Survey response received.
- Agent handoff or low-confidence skip.
- SLA breach risk or breach.
- Client/account activity changes.

## Design requirements before launch

Before webhooks become live, Woes should support:

- Per-workspace endpoint configuration.
- Server-side signing secrets.
- Delivery retries and backoff.
- Delivery logs and replay controls.
- Permission-gated create/update/delete actions.
- Redacted payloads with no secrets, provider internals, or cross-workspace data.
