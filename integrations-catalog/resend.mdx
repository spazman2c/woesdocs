# Resend Email Sending

Resend is the email delivery layer Woes uses for configured support email and survey delivery. In the product catalog, Resend is shown separately from the Email channel because domain verification, DNS records, and sender health are operational setup concerns.

## Status

**Available now.** Woes can create and track email sender domains, show required DNS records, and use verified email configuration for supported email flows.

## What it does

Resend setup helps teams:

- Add the sending domain they want Woes to use.
- Review DNS records required at the DNS provider.
- Verify that the domain is ready before relying on email delivery.
- Keep support email and survey delivery tied to the same Woes workspace.

## Where it appears in Woes

- **Workspace Settings -\> Channels -\> Email** manages sender domain setup and email behavior.
- **Settings -\> Integrations** shows Resend/domain status as part of the integration catalog.
- **Settings -\> Surveys** configures email CSAT and NPS prompts.

## Domain setup checklist

1. Add the exact domain or subdomain you want Woes to send from.
2. Copy the DNS records shown in Woes.
3. Add those records in your DNS provider.
4. Return to Woes and refresh/verify the domain state.
5. Send a controlled test before enabling production survey or support email flows.
