# Broadcast Analytics

Broadcast analytics should answer one question: did the message help customers
understand or act?

## What To Watch

| Signal | What it tells you |
| --- | --- |
| Replies | Customers still have questions or need help. |
| Related conversations | The announcement may have reduced or increased support volume. |
| Related issues | Customers may still be blocked by the same product area. |
| Survey results | Customers may confirm whether follow-up was useful. |
| Docs traffic | Customers may be reading the linked source of truth. |

## Review Workflow

1. Compare related conversation volume before and after the broadcast.
2. Review customer replies for unresolved confusion.
3. Check whether linked docs or product context need another update.
4. Tag follow-up issues consistently.
5. Add findings to the product or support review.

## Better Follow-Ups

If a broadcast creates confusion, send a smaller, clearer follow-up rather than
another broad announcement. Focus on what customers need to do next.
